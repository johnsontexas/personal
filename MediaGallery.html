Christ Crucifixion Using Pen Tool (Adobe Illustrator) ‐ November 2024

We had a project for Illustrator where we had to use the pen tool function to make an art piece. We could choose any photo for reference, and I settled on a depiction of Christ on the crucifix. This is the product of that project.
